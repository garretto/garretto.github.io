Welcome Students & Enthusiasts!

Posted on May 7, 2012 by Garrett

This is a project website for a group who took a Mechatronics course (SEng 466) at the University of Victoria in Victoria, BC. We designed, built, and programmed a quadcopter using Solidworks, a MakerBot 3D printer, and a modified version of the AeroQuad software.

If you have any comments or questions, feel free to leave a comment on this post or anywhere else on this site.

Project 1 details a preliminary project not having to do with the quadcopter.

Project 2 explains most details about the mechanical design of the quadcopter frame.

Project 3 shows some modifications we made to the AeroQuad software and some demonstration videos of the copter flying.

Thanks for visiting!
